Our Story

Built on Trust & Care

Established in 2001, Abhiram Medical Agencies has grown into one of the most trusted pharmaceutical distributors in North Telangana. With over two decades of experience, we serve 800+ retail pharmacies and medical stores across 6 districts — built on a foundation of reliability, integrity, and genuine care for healthcare.

We partner with 150+ leading pharmaceutical companies including Cipla, Sun Pharma, Dr. Reddy's, Abbott, Lupin, and more — ensuring a comprehensive product portfolio is always available for our retail partners.

Rooted in Kamareddy and blessed by divine grace, we are more than a distributor — we are a healthcare partner our community depends on, every single day.
